The weekend of love is coming and Sergi Ferrer has sent us his suggestion.

‘I have never liked croissant horns! Too crunchy and sometimes too darned bitter from spending too long in the oven… My friend John, trend hunter, sent me a picture from Melbourne. At Café Lune they have mixed the concept of a Muffin and a Croissant.

There we have it, they have saved our palate! I decided to knead my raspberries into it.

Be tender with your Valentine! No more bitter days!’
